About Us

Word of Grace Bible Church, Eket, Nigeria was established on December 3, 1998, in Eket, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria, as an expression of God's mandate to our Lead Pastor, George Umana. The Church was formally inaugurated by Pastor Simeon Afolabi, on Saturday, April 7, 2001 at The Banquet Hall, Royalty Hotels, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria.
