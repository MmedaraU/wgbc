About Us

Word of Grace Bible Church

Word of Grace Bible Church, Eket, Nigeria was established on December 3, 1998, in Eket, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria, as an expression of God's mandate to our Lead Pastor, George Umana.

The Church was formally inaugurated by Pastor Simeon Afolabi, on Saturday, April 7, 2001 at The Banquet Hall, Royalty Hotels, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. The foundational Scripture for the Church is 2 Timothy 2:2 while Acts 20:32 inspired the name of the church.

Our Vision

To be a leading Word-based assembly of believers in Christ Jesus building servant leaders to passionately do the work of ministry and exert godly influence in their world.

"And the things that you have heard from me among many witnesses, commit these to faithful men who will be able to teach others also."
2 Timothy 2:2(NKJV)

Our Mission

Building our membership for servant leadership through quality teaching of the word of God's redemptive grace and training in an environment of love, acceptance and discipline, to impact our community and world through a growing influence of our membership in service to God and humanity.

"And the things that you have heard from me among many witnesses, commit these to faithful men who will be able to teach others also."
2 Timothy 2:2(NKJV)
"So now, brethren, I commend you to God and to the word of His grace, which is able to build you up and give you an inheritance among all those who are sanctified."
Acts 20:32(NKJV)

Our Core Values

Worship

We are committed to the exaltation of God, individually and corporately as our highest priority.

Servanthood

We are committed to becoming a community of believers that is earnestly, zealously doing all we can in Christ for each other and others beyond our Church.

The Word

We are committed to promoting biblical education for every person at the place where such a person can access biblical truths.

Evangelism

We are committed to the evangelizing of the non-Christians in our community and world, because we believe that lost people matter to God.

Capacity Building

We are committed to equipping every member of WGBC for ministry in and through the Church.

Community

We are committed to building a community or network of Christ-centred, loving and self-sacrificing relationships where people can love and be loved, know and be known.

Excellence

We are committed to excellence in everything we do in our operations and processes, because we believe that giving our best honours God and inspires people.

Mission

We are committed to world missions in any and every way God allows us to have a part in the great good He is doing in the earth.

Prayer

We are committed to being a Church where prayer is a central focus.
